# Administrative boundary map attribution

The non-Korean result maps are produced from geoBoundaries `gbOpen` source
geometry at revision `9469f09`. KoreaSim converted the fixed GeoJSON inputs to
SVG, projected them for screen display, and simplified the geometry to 2% for
web delivery. Region names and source attribution are retained.

Each derived SVG is distributed under the source terms listed below. No source
organization endorses KoreaSim.

## United States ADM1

- Source: United States Census Bureau, MAF/TIGER Database, republished by geoBoundaries.
- Fixed input: [geoBoundaries-USA-ADM1.geojson](https://github.com/wmgeolab/geoBoundaries/raw/9469f09/releaseData/gbOpen/USA/ADM1/geoBoundaries-USA-ADM1.geojson)
- Terms: [United States Census Bureau public-domain information](https://www.census.gov/about/policies/quality/guidelines/data-protection.html)
- Derived asset: `us-regions.v1.svg`, Public Domain source.
